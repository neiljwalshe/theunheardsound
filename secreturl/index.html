Behind The Scenes

Why am I doing this?
My sister-in-law lost over 150K to the pokies within one year. She was married to my brother and they had three kids. She looked after the family finances, so my brother only found out when he couldn't pay for petrol one morning. She had lost their house deposit, drained their three kids bank accounts, and maxed out all the credit cards. The family soon broke up and it's affected their lives ever since.
